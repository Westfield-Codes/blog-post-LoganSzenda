My Global Glimpse Trip 2024

10/11/24 Logan Szenda

Enticing the presence of the lushus green jungles of Costa Rica, I felt that the plane couldn't land any slower. I have been preparing for this moment since the start of my junior year and finally it was here. When the plane finally landed I set foot into a new world far beyond the one I knew. The first night we stayed in a hotel, which appeared to be much advanced in contrast with the surrounding homes. The next day our bus ride to the house we stayed at felt longer than the plane ride. The mountainous terrain made it difficult to not at least feel queezy.
Arriving at the house our trip began and ended in a flash. The 20 strangers, all the ages of 16-17 became closer than expected. We bonded over music. Laughed at each other's jokes. Ate every meal together. Played games to pass the time. But this was only in the free time we had, otherwise our days were packed with adventure. We visited historic and religous sites in the city Cartago. Ate at various restaraunts with delicous fish, rice and beans, and plantanes.
